# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the ATON community a welcoming, respectful and inclusive experience for everyone.

We value constructive discussions, technical excellence and collaboration.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and professional.
- Providing constructive feedback.
- Focusing discussions on technical topics.
- Respecting differing viewpoints.
- Accepting constructive criticism.
- Helping new contributors.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Personal attacks.
- Harassment or discrimination.
- Trolling or insulting language.
- Publishing private information.
- Deliberately disrupting discussions.

## Our Philosophy

ATON is an engineering project.

Architecture decisions should be discussed based on technical arguments and documented through Architecture Decision Records (ADRs).

Respectful disagreement is encouraged.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Repeated violations may result in removal from the project.
